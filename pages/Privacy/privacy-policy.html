Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 4, 2024

Application Scope

This privacy policy and website tools are specifically designed for the Canadian insurance market. All calculation rules and rate standards are based on provincial insurance regulations, primarily applicable to the following Canadian insurance providers:

  • Intact Insurance
  • Aviva Canada
  • Desjardins Insurance
  • TD Insurance
  • RSA Canada
  • Wawanesa Insurance

1. Introduction

Welcome to Insurance Cancellation Calculator ("we," "our," or "us"). This privacy policy explains how our calculator tools work and how we protect your privacy.

Our website is a pure front-end tool that performs all calculations directly in your browser. We do not collect, store, or process any of your personal information.

2. How Our Tools Work

When you use our calculators and tools:

  • All calculations are performed entirely within your web browser
  • Any information you enter (such as insurance details, policy numbers, or premium amounts) remains on your device
  • No data is sent to our servers or stored in any database
  • Your information is never shared with third parties

3. Cookies and Local Storage

Our website uses minimal cookies and local storage for the following purposes only:

  • Functional Cookies: To remember your cookie consent preference
  • Local Storage: To temporarily store calculator inputs for a better user experience (this data never leaves your device)

You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser cookies and local storage.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website may use the following third-party services:

4.1 Google Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics that help us improve our website. This information is aggregated and does not identify you personally. You can opt out of Google Analytics by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

4.2 Google AdSense

We may display advertisements from Google AdSense. Google AdSense may use cookies to personalize ads. You can learn more about how Google uses information from sites that use their services by visiting Google's Privacy & Terms page.

5. Your Data Protection Rights

Since we do not collect or store your personal data, there is no personal information for us to provide, delete, or modify. All information you enter remains on your device and under your control.

6. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
